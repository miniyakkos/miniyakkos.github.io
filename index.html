About Me

Always curious as to how things work, I wondered, "How does the Internet and its websites work?" I really wanted to understand the "magic" behind the Internet. So I decided to delve into the world of web development by enrolling at Dev Bootcamp. I am a recent graduate of Dev Bootcamp, with proficiency in Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. However, the most valuable skill I gained was learning how to learn. Naturally a fast learner, I improved my efficiency by learning to pinpoint the exact question I need to ask to solve any problem. I am a lifelong learner and excited for the learning opportunities that exist in the tech industry. I also admire the sense of community among people in the industry, especially the spirit of giving back to the community via mentoring or contributing to open source projects.
